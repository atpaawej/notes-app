# Notes App

A personal notebook with authentication, supporting markdown notes organised by tags.

## Language

**User**:
A person with an account who owns notes.
_Avoid_: Member, profile, account

**Note**:
A titled piece of markdown content with a body, owned by a User.
_Avoid_: Document, page, entry, post

**Tag**:
A flat label that can be attached to zero or more Notes. Tags are not hierarchical.
_Avoid_: Category, folder, label, topic
